\section*{Example table}

Below is a two-row, two-column table (2\texttimes 2).

\begin{table}[htbp]
  \centering
  \caption{A 2\texttimes 2 table}
  \label{tab:two-by-two}
  \begin{tabular}{|l|l|}
    \hline
    Row\,1 / Col\,1 & Row\,1 / Col\,2 \\
    \hline
    Row\,2 / Col\,1 & Row\,2 / Col\,2 \\
    \hline
  \end{tabular}
\end{table}

You can omit the floating \texttt{table} wrapper and use only the \texttt{tabular} environment if you prefer the table inlined with the surrounding text:

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{cc}
  \(\alpha\) & \(\beta\) \\
  \(\gamma\) & \(\delta\) \\
\end{tabular}
\end{center}
